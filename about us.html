SJHS DIGITAL LIBRARY VISION & MISSION



The formation of well-informed individuals in the pursuit of knowledge.

1. To provide books and other learning materials that will sustain and intensify reading ability of the students thus encouraging them full utilization of its collections in the acquisition of knowledge. 2. To equip students full of knowledge on the proper use of library and its collections, instilling and inculcating in their young minds the love of books and develop the habit of reading.
